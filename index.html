Apps 4 Spellers

Welcome to Apps 4 Spellers

Mobile applications designed specifically for people with speech and fine motor challenges.

Spell 2 Speak

Spell 2 Speak is designed to help anyone who cannot rely soly on speech for communication. It features a large alphabet keyboard that allows users to communicate by spelling.

Mega Key

Mega Key is a large custom keyboard that can be used in other applications.

Math Composer

Math Composer is an iPad app for writing math equations digitally using a large keyboard with math symbols and operators.
